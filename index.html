Jordan Christensen

My name is Jordan Christensen, and I am an iOS developer. I have had a passion for software for as long as I can remember, and becoming a software engineer has always been my dream. As a 14 year old, I wanted to create my own websites so I looked into website builders. After realizing how little customization website builders had, I decided to switch to HTML and CSS. Later, I wanted to make video games and computer applications so I turned to Java. Now, I am making apps for iOS in Swift and Objective-C.
